My Hobbies

Coding coding is one of my activities when I have free time, with coding my emotional feelings can change, when I succeed in doing something I want I feel happy, and conversely when I fail, I feel restless and want to fix it immediately. I think this activity is very fun

Rubiks Playing Rubik's Cube is a fun activity, I often play it when I'm bored. by playing rubik, i can control stress by shuffling it, this is very satisfying. this can bring back my chaotic mood

Games Playing games is an activity to relieve boredom and stress. By playing the game, I have many very good friends. my favorite game is minecraft

Movies on weekends, homework is done. I spend a lot of time watching my favorite movies & anime. I think this activity can train emotional and relieve fatigue after activities.

Song This is my favorite hobby, songs can affect my mood, when I hear happy songs, I feel happy too, and when I hear sad songs, I also feel sad. by hearing and singing a song, I feel excited.
